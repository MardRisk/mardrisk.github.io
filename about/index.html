About MardRisk

What MardRisk AI is

MardRisk AI is a financial assistant focused on risk clarity. Instead of only showing expenses or generic advice, it tries to explain how risky a user’s financial situation is and why a purchase, loan, or habit may become dangerous.

1

Risk Score

It gives users a fast personal financial risk score based on income, spending pressure, debt, and financial stability.

2

Buy Check

It helps evaluate whether a purchase is safe, risky, or better postponed according to the user’s real situation.

3

Loan Intelligence

It can analyze loans, debt pressure, and repayment burden, then provide clearer recommendations.

4

Multilingual AI Experience

MardRisk AI is designed to work in English, Armenian, and Russian so more users can understand financial risk in their own language.
